Our Story

Providing comfort, care, and a warm learning environment for every child.

Welcome to Dahir Family Childcare

Dahir Family Childcare began with a simple intention: to create a warm, nurturing, and safe space where children feel truly at home. We believe every child deserves individual attention, gentle guidance, and opportunities to grow at their own pace.

We also understand the challenges families face—especially parents who work unconventional or extended hours. Many parents struggle to find reliable nighttime or early-morning care, which is why we chose to offer flexible and dependable support around the clock.

Over time, our childcare home has grown into a place filled with curiosity, creativity, and joyful learning. We designed our environment intentionally: small, safe, structured, and welcoming. Each day is centered around building confidence, encouraging exploration, and helping children feel seen and supported.

We proudly offer 24/7 child care for families with rotating shifts, late-night jobs, early-morning responsibilities, or irregular schedules. Whether your child needs daytime, evening, or overnight care, we are here to provide stability and comfort.

Our mission going forward is to continue being a reliable, trusted, and loving environment where children grow emotionally, socially, and intellectually—while supporting parents with the flexibility they deserve.
